University Management System

UniMS is a web digital solution that helps automate tasks. Ranging from registering students, admitting students, onboarding them, managing their fees, managing payroll for lecturers to helping students access online learning, classroom training etc. a university management system is a complete solution. Reducing manual labor and intervention, this system ensures accuracy, transparency, reliability, and integrity of records, information, intellectual property and data.
